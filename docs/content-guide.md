# Content Guide

## Tone

Use a tone that is:

- practical
- honest
- simple
- reflective where useful
- not overhyped
- not too technical unless needed

## Avoid

Avoid phrases that sound like:

- full-service digital agency
- guaranteed leads
- guaranteed jobs
- AI expert for everything
- digital transformation
- growth hacking

## Prefer

Use simple phrases like:

- simple website
- explanation page
- shareable link
- clear offer
- project plan
- MVP scope
- contact form
- practical guidance

## Blog Post Shape

Recommended structure:

```markdown
# Title

Short intro.

## The problem

## Why it matters

## Practical breakdown

## Example

## What to do next

## Related service/resource
```
